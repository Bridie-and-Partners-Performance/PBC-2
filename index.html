Who We Are

Fostering Prosperity for Palestine and Palestinians

PBC is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Palestinian business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals in the United Arab Emirates, as well as forging and cementing economic and business ties between the UAE and the State of Palestine.

We bring together a vibrant community of leaders, innovators, and change-makers united by a shared commitment to growth and excellence.

Our Foundation

Purpose · Vision · Mission

🎯

Our Purpose

To foster prosperity for Palestine and Palestinians — creating economic bridges, building capacity, and empowering a generation of business leaders in the UAE and beyond.

🔭

Our Vision

To be a globally connected and impactful Palestinian business community, in and through the UAE — recognised for excellence, integrity, and meaningful contribution.

🚀

Our Mission

Empowering the Palestinian business community to connect, grow, and create meaningful impact through strategic collaboration, advocacy, capacity building, and knowledge sharing.
